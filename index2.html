Who Are We?

We Are Africare

Africare is a global Non-Profit organization and Trust Fund, founded in 2020 after the initial covid-19 outbreak by its well-meaning, philanthropic and humanitarian pioneers, for the cause of tackling ignorance and its malaises, and limitations brought about due to the lack of technical know-how on the aspect of Information Technology. And also for the empowering of innovative and powerful ideas of African Children born without the option of a chance or opportunity.
