Who Are We?

We Are Africare

Africare is a global Non-Profit organization and Trust Fund, founded in 2020 after the initial covid-19 outbreak by its well-meaning, philanthropic and humanitarian pioneers, for the cause of tackling ignorance and its malaises, limitations due to the lack of technical know-how on the aspect of Information Technology. And the empowering of innovative and powerful ideas of African Children born without the option of a chance or opportunity. We give audience to every African Child and create a platform for success, which levels the playing field for all individuals, by offering basic and beginner courses and services that close the gap between the privileged and the under privileged. Africare aims at the breaking of mental and physical strongholds of traditional paralysis and cultural limitations and dichotomy, that necessarily, and sometimes, if left unchecked, gives birth to many other social injustices and anomalies. Gender inequality and the passing on of generational holdbacks.

Mission Statements

Empowering the African Child

A popular maxim asserts “live and let others”. We are driven to bring this to life for the African Child. Tales are told of unheard living conditions and terrible outlooks for African Children, we bridge the gap between; in terms of opportunities and sponsorship, for the talents and ideas born in Africa and the World’s average.

Creating a Tomorrow for Children

Humanity all over the world is dealt a different hand, some worse than the other. It is our target and mission to bring hope and enlightenment to less privileged Children, especially after the havoc and instability wreaked by the effects of covid-19, and the obvious ineptitude that can be seen in this part of the world, most notably, at forging ahead and catching up to the rest of the world in crisis management.

Liberating peoples from Traditional, Cultural, Mental and Geographical Paralysis

Knowledge they say is power; power indeed is the capability to decide and dictate to a great extent the outcome of one’s tomorrow. The World’s problems of Hunger, Flood, Drought, Insecurity, Crime, Terrorism, can all be helped by giving people the tools that would aid them liberate themselves. As much as we provide immediate help of different kinds, we rather choose to be more proactive, and instill the tools for achieving those same standards within the Children.
